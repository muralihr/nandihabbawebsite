RECYCLING AND GREEN ISSUES

Recycling and sustainability are a cornerstone of Nandi Habba's principles.We always aim to choose sustainable materials and locally sourced products, thus dramatically reducing impact on the environment.

Recycling bins are dotted around the site to contribute to keeping the hills/ grounds in pristine condition, hence leaving no trace.

Reusable ‘eco cups’ are used to cut down on plastic waste at the stations and stalls .

All toilets on site are compost toilets. Composting toilets treat human poo using biological processes, turning it into organic compost material that can be used to fertilise the soil. They are small-scale, complete waste processing systems. They typically break down waste material to a small percentage of its original volume.
